Goals

The goal of the website will be to bring the Barrier Reef Orchestra into the modern world, by doing this we hope to appeal to a younger audience and increase revenue for the client.

Success Evaluation

The success of the page will be evaluated by three success criteria ; donations, members joining and event attendance.

Target Audience

The targeted audience for the website will be people between the ages of 15-25 years old.

We will design our website so that is basic but interactive. Example - Hovering over a picture reveals more information.
